About Western States Productions

Western States Productions is the leading organizer of ranch sorting competitions across the Western United States. Founded by industry veterans, we bring together the best riders, horses, and cattle for unforgettable events that celebrate cowboy culture and horsemanship.

Our mission is to promote the sport of ranch sorting while preserving the western traditions and values that make this community special. We prioritize fair competition, animal welfare, and creating a welcoming environment for participants of all skill levels.

Ride Like Wyatt Program

Our youth initiative dedicated to introducing the next generation to the sport of ranch sorting and western horsemanship. Through clinics, scholarships, and competitive opportunities, we're ensuring the cowboy tradition continues for years to come.
